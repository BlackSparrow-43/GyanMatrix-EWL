TESLA

  1. Tesla has its own charging network, exclusive to Tesla owners.
  2. Other EVs (like Rivian and Jeep) are starting to build out their own networks as well, but none are as extensive as Tesla's.
  3. The fast charging adds up to 200 miles in 15 minutes.
  4. Supercharging is available across the U.S. and in other countries where Teslas are sold.
    • No longer free like it was in the early days of Tesla
    • you either pay by the minute or kilowatt using a credit card loaded into the car computer.
    • And There are more than 25,000 superchargers.
